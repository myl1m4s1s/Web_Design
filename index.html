About Us

The Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology (MCAST) is a hub of learning and innovation. We offer a wide range of courses in various disciplines including engineering, business, technology, arts, and social sciences. Our mission is to empower students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in their chosen fields, whether they pursue further education or dive straight into the workforce.

With dedicated staff, modern facilities, and a vibrant student community, MCAST is a dynamic institution that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.
